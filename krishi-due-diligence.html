Due Diligence for Krishi

Project Overview

As part of my role with the Purdue Venture Capital Club, I conducted due diligence for Krishi, a company focused on providing on-demand molecular insights for veterinary care. While specific details of the pitch deck are confidential due to an NDA, this project provided valuable experience in venture analysis, market research, and strategic evaluation.

About Krishi

Krishi's mission is to revolutionize veterinary care through innovative solutions like the SHERPA™ platform, which provides lab-accurate antibiotic guidance for urinary tract and skin infections in pets. The platform consists of two components:

  • SHERPA™ Vision: A reusable, IoT-connected device for imaging and analysis.
  • SHERPA™ Chip: A consumable test with embedded reagents for quick and accurate results.
Krishi's technology aims to enhance antimicrobial stewardship, speed up patient recovery, and augment veterinary practice revenue.

Key Learnings

This due diligence process provided valuable insights and skills, including:

  • Market Analysis: Conducted in-depth research on the veterinary diagnostics market, including trends, competitors, and customer needs.
  • Financial Evaluation: Analyzed Krishi's business model, revenue streams, and growth potential.
  • Technical Assessment: Evaluated the feasibility and scalability of Krishi's SHERPA™ platform.
  • Strategic Insights: Identified key opportunities and risks for Krishi's market entry and expansion.

Conclusion

The due diligence process for Krishi was an invaluable learning experience, providing hands-on exposure to venture analysis and strategic decision-making. While specific findings are confidential, the project enhanced my skills in market research, financial modeling, and technical evaluation, preparing me for future roles in venture capital and business strategy.
