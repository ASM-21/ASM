Due Diligence for Krishi

Project Overview

As part of my role with Purdue's Student Managed Venture Fund course, I conducted comprehensive due diligence for Krishi, an innovative company focused on providing on-demand molecular insights for veterinary care. This engagement provided invaluable experience in venture analysis, market research, and strategic evaluation. Information was excluded due to a NDA.

About Krishi

Krishi's mission is to revolutionize veterinary care through their innovative SHERPA™ platform, which provides lab-accurate antibiotic guidance for urinary tract and skin infections in pets. The platform consists of two complementary components:

  • SHERPA™ Vision: A reusable, IoT-connected device that performs advanced imaging and analysis to identify pathogens rapidly.
  • SHERPA™ Chip: A consumable test with embedded reagents that delivers quick and accurate diagnostic results.

Krishi's technology aims to enhance antimicrobial stewardship, accelerate patient recovery, and significantly augment veterinary practice revenue through point-of-care diagnostics.

Key Learnings

This comprehensive due diligence process provided valuable insights and developed critical skills, including:

  • Market Analysis: Conducted in-depth research on the veterinary diagnostics market, including trends, competitive landscape, and unmet customer needs.
  • Financial Evaluation: Analyzed Krishi's business model, revenue streams, unit economics, and long-term growth potential.
  • Technical Assessment: Evaluated the technical feasibility, scalability, and competitive advantages of Krishi's SHERPA™ platform.
  • Strategic Insights: Identified key opportunities, potential risks, and critical success factors for Krishi's market entry and expansion strategy.

Conclusion

The due diligence process for Krishi was an invaluable learning experience, providing hands-on exposure to venture analysis and strategic decision-making in the rapidly evolving veterinary technology sector. While specific findings remain confidential, this project significantly enhanced our skills in market research, financial modeling, and technical evaluation, preparing us for future roles in venture capital and business strategy.

Working as a team allowed us to leverage diverse perspectives and expertise, resulting in a comprehensive analysis that considered technical, financial, and market factors.
