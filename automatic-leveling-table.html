Automatic Underwater Sample Leveling Table

Overview

The Automatic Underwater Sample Leveling Table is designed to streamline ultrasonic nondestructive evaluation (NDE) by automating the leveling process. Currently, manual leveling takes up to an hour and is prone to human error. This project uses stepper motors and lead screws to adjust the table's tilt, ensuring the sample is level within a 15 µm tolerance in under 5 minutes.

Key Features

  • Precision Leveling: Achieves ±0.5° precision using stepper motors and lead screws.
  • Waterproof Design: IP68-rated stepper motors and waterproof connectors ensure safe operation underwater.
  • Feedback Loop: Utilizes ultrasonic scanner feedback to adjust the table's tilt automatically.
  • Compatibility: Fits within the existing ultrasonic testing setup and supports samples up to 15 lbs.

Technical Details

The system consists of a triangular table with two motorized corners and one stationary corner. Stepper motors rotate lead screws to adjust the table's height, while a microcontroller processes feedback from the ultrasonic scanner to ensure precise leveling. The design minimizes mechanical disturbance in the water and operates on standard 120 VAC power.

Results

The prototype is expected to reduce leveling time from 1 hour to under 5 minutes, improving the efficiency and accuracy of ultrasonic NDE. The system will be tested in a controlled underwater environment and presented at the National Engineering Symposium.
